Mac n Beef

Description

One of my favorite meals growing up whenever I saw my grandpa. If you like pasta and cheeseburgers, you're gonna love this one. Turns out I can make it for myself now, it's actually relatively easy to make, delicious, and filling.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni pasta
  • 1 1/4 pound ground beef
  • 1 1/4 pound white American cheese slices
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika

Steps

  1. Pan fry the ground beef with some oil.
  2. Season the beef to taste with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  3. Boil the pasta.
  4. In a bowl or another pan, layer pasta, beef, cheese, and repeat many times.
  5. Mix it all together, and serve!
