Berry Yummy Smoothie

Description

Hopefully, the best damn smoothie you'll ever have. It's a fruit and yogurt smoothie that blends all your favorite ingredients into one healthy, delicious drink. Add whatever fruits and veggies you like and truly make it your own.

Ingredients

  • Frozen berries
  • Frozen tropical fruit
  • Frozen chopped spinach
  • Plain Greek yogurt
  • Orange juice
  • Water
  • Flax seed/meal
  • Chia seeds

Steps

  1. Put all the frozen fruit and veggies into the blender first.
  2. Put whatever mix of orange juice and water you like in.
  3. Add some yogurt.
  4. Add any extra stuff you want, such as flax, chia, acai, protein powder, etc.
  5. Blend it all together.
  6. Try it, and adjust for taste and thickness to your liking.
